Hello, World!

The world is full of interesting facts that surprise us every day. Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood, while honey never spoils and has been found edible in ancient tombs. A single lightning bolt is five times hotter than the surface of the Sun. Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not. Your brain generates enough electricity to power a small light bulb. There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth. Sharks existed before trees, and wombats produce cube-shaped poop. These fascinating facts remind us how strange and amazing our planet truly is.
